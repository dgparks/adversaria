## English

*Use human means as though divine ones didn’t exist, and divine means as though there were no human ones.* A great master* gave that advice, and it requires no comment.

---
> *Saint Ignatius of Loyola (1491-1556), founder of the Society of Jesus.

## Spanish

*Hanse de procurar los medios humanos como si no huviesse divinos, y los divinos como si no huviesse humanos*. Regla de gran maestro; no ai que añadir comento.
