## English

*Beware of someone who pretends to put your interest before his own.* The best defence against guile is attentiveness. When people are subtle, be even more so. Some are good at making their business into yours, and if you don’t decipher their intentions, you’ll ﬁnd yourself pulling their chestnuts out of the ﬁre.

## Spanish

*Atención al que entra con la agena por salir con la suya*. No ai reparo para la astucia como la advertencia. Al entendido, un buen entendedor. Hazen algunos ageno el negocio proprio, y sin la contraçifra de intenciones se halla a cada passo empeñado uno en sacar del fuego el provecho ageno con daño de su mano.
